# Privacy Policy for Medicinder

**Last Updated:** December 11, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Medicinder ("the App," "we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our medication management application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Medication Information:**
- Medication names, types, and dosages
- Prescription schedules and timing
- Meal-based medication scheduling information
- Treatment duration and instructions
- Usage notes and special instructions

**Personal Data:**
- No personally identifiable information (such as name, email, or phone number) is required to use the App unless you voluntarily provide it

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- App version
- Time zone settings

**Usage Information:**
- How you interact with the App
- Features you access
- Frequency of use
- Notification preferences and responses

**Local Storage:**
- All medication data is stored locally on your device using Hive database
- Notification history and reminders

### 2.3 Permissions Used

The App requires the following permissions to function properly:

- **Notification Permission:** Required to send medication reminders and alerts
- **Device Calendar (if available):** Used for scheduling medication reminders
- **Local Storage Access:** Required to store medication data locally on your device

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

- **Provide Core Functionality:** Display and manage your medications and schedules
- **Send Notifications:** Deliver medication reminders at scheduled times
- **Improve the App:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
- **Localization:** Provide content in your preferred language (English or Arabic)
- **Compliance:** Fulfill legal and regulatory requirements
- **Technical Support:** Troubleshoot issues and provide customer support

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

- **On-Device Storage:** All medication data is stored locally on your device using Hive database
- **No Cloud Sync:** We do not transmit your medication data to external servers by default
- **Offline Access:** Your data remains accessible even without an internet connection

### 4.2 Security Measures

- Your medication data remains private to your device
- We implement industry-standard security practices
- Your data does not require transmission encryption because it remains stored locally on your device and is not sent over the internet.
- We recommend keeping your device secure with appropriate unlock methods

### 4.3 Data Retention

- Medication data is stored locally on your device until you manually delete it
- You can delete individual medications or clear all data from the App settings
- Uninstalling the App may result in loss of data depending on your device settings

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We do **not** share your medication data with third parties, including:
- Advertisers
- Analytics companies (unless anonymized)
- Social networks
- Healthcare providers (unless you manually export or share)

### 5.2 Exceptions

We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to:
- Valid legal requests or court orders
- Law enforcement inquiries
- Protection of rights, privacy, or safety
- Prevention of fraud or security issues

### 5.3 Business Transfers

If Medicinder is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## 6. Third-Party Services

### 6.1 Dependencies and Libraries

Our App uses the following open-source libraries that may collect or process data:

- **awesome_notifications:** Used for sending local notifications (no data transmission)
- **shared_preferences:** Used for storing user preferences locally
- **hive:** Local database for medication data storage
- **flutter_bloc:** App state management (local only)
- **get_it:** Dependency injection (local only)

### 6.2 No Analytics Tracking

We do not integrate third-party analytics services that track your personal data by default. Any analytics we may implement in the future will be:
- Anonymized and aggregated
- Limited to app performance metrics
- Clearly disclosed in an updated privacy policy

## 7. Children's Privacy

Medicinder is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

**Parental Guidance:** If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our App, please contact us immediately.
