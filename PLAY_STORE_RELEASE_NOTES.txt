🎯 WHY MEDICINDER?

✅ RELIABLE REMINDERS
Never forget to take your medication with smart, persistent notifications that work even when your phone is locked or the app is closed.

✅ PRIVACY FIRST
All your medication data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, share, or store your personal information. Your health data stays private.

✅ EASY TO USE
Simple, intuitive interface that makes managing multiple medications effortless. No complicated setup or learning curve required.

✅ SMART & EFFICIENT
Optimized for battery life and performance. The app works smoothly in the background without draining your battery.

✅ MULTILINGUAL SUPPORT
Fully localized in English and Arabic with proper RTL (Right-to-Left) layout support for Arabic users.

🕐 SMART MEDICATION SCHEDULING

• SPECIFIC TIME SCHEDULING
  Set exact times for your medication doses (e.g., 8:00 AM, 2:00 PM, 8:00 PM)

• MEAL-BASED SCHEDULING
  Schedule medications relative to meals:
  - Before breakfast, lunch, or dinner
  - After breakfast, lunch, or dinner
  - Customizable time offsets for each meal

• FLEXIBLE TIMING
  Adjust meal times in settings to match your daily routine
  Set custom offsets (e.g., 30 minutes before breakfast)

• DURATION TRACKING
  Set treatment duration in days
  Automatic completion detection when treatment courses finish
  Medications automatically move to "Taken" screen when all doses are completed

📋 HOW TO USE

1. ADD YOUR MEDICATIONS
   Tap the + button to add a new medication
   Enter medication name, usage, dosage, and schedule

2. SET YOUR SCHEDULE
   Choose specific times or meal-based scheduling
   Set treatment duration in days

3. GET REMINDERS
   Receive timely notifications with action buttons
   Mark doses as taken directly from notifications

4. TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
   View active medications on the home screen
   Swipe to see your taken history with timestamps
   Medications automatically organize when completed

5. MANAGE EASILY
   Edit or delete medications anytime
   Adjust meal times in settings
   Switch languages as needed
